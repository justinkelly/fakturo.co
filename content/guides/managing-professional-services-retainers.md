## Practical Guide: Managing Professional Services Retainers

Managing your business should be simple. This guide walks you through **Managing Professional Services Retainers** using Fakturo's intuitive interface.

### Step 1: Configuration
Ensure your tax ID (NIF/CIF) is correctly set in your profile.

### Step 2: Execution
Select the client and apply the correct Spanish VAT (IVA) rate (4%, 10%, or 21%).
