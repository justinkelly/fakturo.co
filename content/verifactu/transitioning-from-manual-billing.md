## The Future of Invoicing: Transitioning from Manual Billing

The Spanish government has introduced the Veri*factu* system to fight fraud and digitalize the economy. **Transitioning from Manual Billing** is a core pillar of this transition.

### Technical Requirements
- **Unalterability:** No more manual deletion of invoices.
- **Traceability:** Every record is linked to the previous one via chained hashes.
- **Connectivity:** Real-time data exchange with the Tax Agency.
