## The Future of Invoicing: Digital Kit Funding for SMEs

The Spanish government has introduced the Veri*factu* system to fight fraud and digitalize the economy. **Digital Kit Funding for SMEs** is a core pillar of this transition.

### Technical Requirements
- **Unalterability:** No more manual deletion of invoices.
- **Traceability:** Every record is linked to the previous one via chained hashes.
- **Connectivity:** Real-time data exchange with the Tax Agency.
