## Turn Your Data Into Actionable Business Intelligence

Don't wait until the end of the year to know how your business is performing. Fakturo's **Financial Reporting** suite gives you real-time visibility into your revenue, expenses, and tax liabilities, helping you make smarter, more informed decisions every day.

### 1. Dynamic Performance Dashboards
Get an instant snapshot of your business health. Our intuitive dashboards track your most important KPIs, including total revenue, net profit, and average payment times. See your growth trends at a glance with beautiful, interactive charts.

Plan for your tax obligations with confidence. Fakturo provides real-time estimates of your VAT (IVA) and income tax (IRPF) liabilities based on your current year-to-date performance. No more surprises when it's time to file.

### 4. Advanced Customization & Export
- **Custom Date Ranges:** Analyze any specific period of time with ease.
- **Client & Project Filters:** Drill down into the performance of specific clients or individual projects.
- **Easy Export:** Download any report in PDF or CSV format for your gestor or for your own offline analysis.

### Business Advantages
- **Better Decision Making:** Use hard data, not intuition, to guide your growth.
- **Stay Tax Ready:** Always know how much to set aside for your quarterly tax filings.
- **Improve Cash Flow:** Identify billing bottlenecks and high-cost areas before they become a problem.
