## Complete Control Over Your Invoicing Lifecycle

Take the complexity out of billing with Fakturo's **Invoicing Management** system. From draft creation to automated follow-ups and final payment reconciliation, manage your entire invoicing workflow in one central dashboard.

### 1. Intuitive Invoice Builder
Create professional, tax-compliant invoices in seconds. Our builder automatically handles Spanish tax requirements (IVA, IRPF, and IGIC) while allowing you to customize line items, discounts, and personalized notes for your clients.

### 2. Automated Status Tracking
Never lose track of an unpaid invoice again. Our dashboard provides real-time visibility into the status of every bill:
- **Draft:** Work on invoices before they are sent.
- **Sent:** Track when clients receive their bills.
- **Paid:** Monitor incoming payments and revenue.
- **Overdue:** Identify and follow up on late payments automatically.

### 3. Smart Document Management
Fakturo organizes your invoices into searchable, filterable lists. Export your data for accounting, attach supporting documents, and maintain a complete audit trail for compliance with Spanish tax laws.

### 4. Advanced Compliance Features
- **Sequential Numbering:** Automatically maintain legal invoice sequences (Series).
- **Tax Verification:** Built-in checks ensure your tax rates are valid for your client's location and business type.
- **Immutable Records:** Secure, AEAT-compliant archiving of all issued invoices.

### Business Advantages
- **Get Paid Faster:** Automated tracking and reminders reduce payment delays.
- **Reduce Paperwork:** Centralized digital storage eliminates manual filing.
- **Total Compliance:** Rest easy knowing your billing meets all current Spanish regulations.
